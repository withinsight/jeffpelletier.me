Conferences

Clarity 2021

Clarity '21 was my introduction to higher-level design systems operation. My second Nathan Curtis workshop spanned two days, and covered planning initiatives & roadmaps, communicating, support, process, contributions, and metrics, amother other ops-level topics.

UI23 Boston 2018

UI23 Boston was my introduction to design systems at scale. Over the course of a full day, Nathan Curtis' Scalable Design Systems workshop introduced participants to planning, designing & building, documenting, and practicing enterprise design systems.

I was introduced to the full lifecycle of design systems at scale. Some topics included:

  • Defining what a design system means for your org
  • Defining teams
  • Design tokens
  • Component inventory exercises
  • How to track design system team progress
  • Aligning API decisions across design & engineering
  • Information architecture for docs sites
  • How to decide what goes in a system
  • and metrics for measuring success

The full gamut!
